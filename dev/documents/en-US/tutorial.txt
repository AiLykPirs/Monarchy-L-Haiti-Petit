Monarchy L'Haiti Petit — Quick Start Guide
============================================

Step 1: Start a Game
1. Click "New Game" on the title screen.
2. Read the prologue and click "Begin Journey".
3. Choose a starting character, enter a name and player ID, then confirm.

Step 2: The Interface
- Top bar: Shows organization name, level, Mandate of Heaven, and other
  core information.
- Left panel (Log): Records all game events and action results.
- Center (Character List): Displays "Free Characters" and "Worker Characters"
  tabs. Click a character to view details and assign actions.
- Right panel (Action Panel): Choose an action for the selected character.
- Bottom panel (Overview): Contains "Court" (ministers), "Harem" (spouses/
  lovers), "Unavailable Characters", and "Dead Characters" tabs.

Step 3: Playing the Game
1. Each year, assign actions for all your characters.
2. After all characters have acted, click "Next Year →" to advance time.
3. Characters grow, age, marry, have children, and carry on through
   generations.
4. Watch your Mandate of Heaven — when it reaches zero, the game ends.

Step 4: Governing the Nation
- Appoint a Chancellor, General, and Overseer to boost organization
  attributes.
- Use "Govern" to raise Technology or Culture, "Drill" to raise Military,
  and "Squeeze" to raise Production or Population.
- Keep the treasury well-funded; tax when necessary.
- Balance popular support against calamity.

Step 5: Conquest & Expansion
- Map targets must be conquered one by one.
- Conquest requires validating 7 conditions, each with a budget allocation.
- Checks are resolved by d100 rolls against target values.
- Passing all key conditions means victory and an organization level-up.

Step 6: Tips & Tricks
- Save often (at least every 10 years).
- Train an heir to ensure bloodline continuity.
- Use "Study"/"Learn Art" to raise character attributes.
- Arrange marriages to bring in new bloodlines and talent.
- When the Mandate is low, use granary policies or adjust governance to
  raise popular support.
