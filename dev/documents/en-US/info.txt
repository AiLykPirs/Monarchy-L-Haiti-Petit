Monarchy L'Haiti Petit — 海地老皇帝
======================================

【Setting】
In the land of Little Haiti, before a new order takes shape, nothing exists but
decay and corruption. But you are different — a visionary destined to lead,
to conquer, and to write your legend in blood and tears upon this fabled soil.
Build your organization from a single office, expanding to a street, a town,
a county, a province, and finally a nation.

【Core Mechanics】
- Role-playing: Each character has 12 attributes (Intelligence, Charisma,
  Stamina, Sexual Ability, Physique, Lifespan, Assertiveness, Wealth,
  Luck, Calamity, Education, Experience). Characters age, marry, have
  children, and pass on through generations.
- Action System: Each year, assign one action per character (Govern, Teach,
  Drill, Squeeze, Study, Exercise, Labor, Date, Rest, Retire, etc.).
  Characters are marked as acted after their action is complete.
- Court & Harem: The Chancellor governs, the General commands the army,
  the Overseer manages production, and the Empress/Consorts maintain the
  bloodline.
- Mandate of Heaven: Popular support (derived from stats and wealth) minus
  calamity (from character misfortune) equals the Mandate. When the Mandate
  reaches zero, the game ends.
- Conquest System: Capturing targets on the map requires validating 7
  conditions (Technology, Culture, Production, Population, Military,
  Popular Support, Treasury). Each condition requires budget allocation
  and a skill check. All key conditions must pass for victory.

【Goals】
- Short-term: Develop organization attributes, recruit talent, stabilize
  your regime.
- Mid-term: Conquer neighboring targets, raise your organization level
  (Office → Street → Town → County → Province → Nation).
- Long-term: Perpetuate your bloodline through dynastic succession and
  build a lasting legacy.
