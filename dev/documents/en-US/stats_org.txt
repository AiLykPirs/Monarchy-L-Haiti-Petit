Organization Stats Reference
=============================

Your organization has 10 attributes that determine the nation's development
and stability. They are recalculated annually at year start.

──────────────────────────────────────

1. Technology (tec)
──────────────────────────────────────
Formula: Base = emp.int + avg(int) x (1 + n/10)
         Total = Base + extraTec + accumulatedTec
Affected by:
  · Up: Scholars auto-work +1/year, great success +1, extreme +d(4)
  · Up: "Invest" policy allocates budget to technology
  · Down: Target territories fluctuate randomly
Influences:
  · Popular Support formula: 0.2 weighting
  · Mandate of Heaven indirectly through Popular Support
  · One of 7 conquest conditions

──────────────────────────────────────

2. Culture (cul)
──────────────────────────────────────
Formula: Base = emp.cha + avg(cha) x (1 + n/10)
         Total = Base + extraCul + accumulatedCul
Affected by:
  · Up: Artists auto-work +1/year, great success +1, extreme +d(4)
  · Up: "Invest" policy allocates budget to culture
  · Down: Target territories fluctuate; govern effects expire
Influences:
  · Popular Support formula: 0.2 weighting
  · Mandate of Heaven indirectly through Popular Support
  · One of 7 conquest conditions

──────────────────────────────────────

3. Production (prd)
──────────────────────────────────────
Formula: Base = overseer.sta + avg(sta) x (1 + n/10)
         Total = Base + extraPrd + accumulatedPrd
Affected by:
  · Up: Laborers auto-work +1/year, success +d(4), great +d(6)
  · Up: "Invest" policy allocates budget
  · Up: "Squeeze" action temporarily raises production
  · Down: Squeeze effects expire; target territories fluctuate
Influences:
  · Popular Support formula: 0.2 weighting
  · One of 7 conquest conditions

──────────────────────────────────────

4. Population (pop)
──────────────────────────────────────
Formula: Base = overseer.sex x 0.5 + avg(sex) x 0.5 + 2n
         Total = Base + extraPop + accumulatedPop
Affected by:
  · Up: Govern success (great/extreme) raises population
  · Up: "Invest" policy allocates budget; all non-emperor chars gain sex+1
  · Up: Pushi concubines contribute +1 population/year
  · Up: "Squeeze (pop)" temporarily raises population
  · Down: Character deaths reduce n, lowering the base
Influences:
  · Popular Support formula: 0.2 weighting
  · One of 7 conquest conditions

──────────────────────────────────────

5. Military (mil)
──────────────────────────────────────
Formula: Base = (emp.psq+emp.con)x0.25 + (general.psq+general.con)x0.25
         + avg(psq)x(1+n/10) + n_soldiers
         Total = Base + extraMil + accumulatedMil
Affected by:
  · Up: Soldiers auto-work +2/year, success +1, great +d(4)
  · Up: "Invest" policy allocates budget
  · Up: "Drill" action temporarily raises military
  · Down: Drill effects expire
Influences:
  · Popular Support formula: 0.2 weighting
  · Conquest: if player mil > opp mil, opponent's check is penalized -d(10)
  · One of 7 conquest conditions

──────────────────────────────────────

6. Popular Support (inf)
──────────────────────────────────────
Formula: Base = 0.2 x (tec+cul+prd+pop+mil)
         + 0.7 x emp.con
         + 0.125 x (empress+chancellor+overseer+general con sum)
         + tre/7
         Total = Base + extraInf + accumulatedInf
Affected by:
  · Up: Artists auto-work raises extra popular support
  · Up: Rising production stats indirectly raise the base
Influences:
  · Mandate of Heaven = 500 - penalty + restorer + inf - apo
  · Conquest: if player inf > opp inf, player's own check is reduced -d(10)

──────────────────────────────────────

7. Treasury (tre)
──────────────────────────────────────
Meaning: National funds. Split into baseTreasury (btre, persistent) and
         extraTreasury (etre, resets to 0 each year).
Formula: Total = btre + etre
Affected by:
  · Up: Tax, Kill/Exile confiscation, Donations, Squeeze (production),
    Laborer/Scholar auto-work
  · Up: Year-end settlement transfers etre to btre
  · Down: Granary policy, Invest policy, Squeeze/Drill/Teach costs,
    Restorer stipends
Influences:
  · Popular Support formula: +tre/7
  · One of 7 conquest conditions

──────────────────────────────────────

8. Calamity (apo)
──────────────────────────────────────
Formula: Base = emp.cal + avg(cal) x (0.2 + n/10)
         Total = Base + extraCalamity + accumulatedCalamity
Affected by:
  · Up: Tax policy raises calamity based on rate
  · Up: Pushi concubines raise calamity annually
  · Rising emperor calamity indirectly raises it
Influences:
  · Mandate of Heaven = 500 - penalty + restorer + inf - apo

──────────────────────────────────────

9. Mandate of Heaven (mdt)
──────────────────────────────────────
Meaning: The dynasty's divine right to rule. Game over when it reaches 0.
Formula: Mandate = 500 - penaltyAccum + restorerAccum + inf - apo
         penaltyAccum increased by Exile, Kill, Coup, Dynasty change, etc.
         restorerAccum increased by Restorers annually
Affected by:
  · Up: Rising Popular Support, falling Calamity, Restorers
  · Down: Exile (+10~80), Kill (+80), Coup (+100), Dynasty change (+100),
    Squeeze (pop), Concubine appointment, Assassination
  · Granary policy directly raises Mandate
  · Assassination directly reduces Mandate
Influences:
  · Mandate <= 0 triggers game over
  · Conquest: if player mdt > opp mdt, opponent's stats take damage

──────────────────────────────────────

10. Level (lvl)
──────────────────────────────────────
Meaning: Organizational administrative level.
Level names: Office(0) → Street(1) → Town(2) → County(3) → Province(4) → Nation(5)
Requirements:
  · Conquer all territory targets to gain 1 level
  · Conquer all administrative targets to gain 1 level
Influences:
  · Displays the organization's prestige and development stage
  · Higher levels unlock stronger talents and spouses
